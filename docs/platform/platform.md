# Platform

Platform refers to the online services hosted by Win93.XYZ that can be accessed through your Win93 ID.

## App store

Apps are submitted to the registry through the *Dev Portal*, which can be accessed through the App Store.
Make sure to read the [app store guidelines](https://win93.xyz/guidelines) before submitting your app.
Apps undergo automatic and/or manual review before being published to the app store. Most apps are published within 48 hours.

All app information is stored in your app manifest.

## Additional properties
The Platform supports additional properties that can be added to your app manifest. Here are the required properties:

- name
- version
- description
- icon
- command
- categories
